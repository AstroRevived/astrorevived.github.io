Introducing AstroOS

AstroOS is a stripped down version of Windows.

Why AstroOS

Windows is slow, clunky and unreliable. AstroOS brings life back to your Windows system, designed to maximize performance and minimize latency. You will experience a smoother and more responsive Windows, and higher framerates over using standard Windows.

No malware. At all.

We've tested our product with several antivirus software, including Malwarebytes, Bitdefender, and Avast, and it's safe to say that AstroOS is malware-free.
